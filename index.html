How it works

How it works.

It's slow on purpose. Writing a letter isn't the same as posting a confession. You both get time to think.

1.

Write a letter

Open a sheet of stationery. Write something you can't easily say out loud. One page is plenty.

2.

Pick who reads it

Gender, life stage, what they've been through. Your letter goes to one person, not a feed.

3.

A reply comes back

Usually within a day or two. Sometimes longer. You'll get a notification when it lands.
